Magnus Number Estimator

More Info  Hide Info 

  • Unofficial personal project. Not associated with Chess.com.
  • Attempts to find any Chess.com user's Magnus Number - a chain of victories by which they "defeated" GM Magnus Carlsen.
  • Not necessarily shortest path. Your true Magnus Number may be lower. This simple estimate is the best I can do with chess.com's public API.
  • Dead ends may occur, despite best efforts to backtrack / avoid loops.
  • Requires a modern browser.
  • This site respects your privacy - no tracking or other cookies used.
  • Feedback & Bug reports: contact me on Reddit
  • Not open source. I prefer to retain sole authorship, as this is my first completed website. Anyone is of course welcome to implement the same idea. The full source is inspectable on  Github
